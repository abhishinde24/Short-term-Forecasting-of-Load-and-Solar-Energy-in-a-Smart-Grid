About Us

We are final year electrical students from VJTI Mumbai and this is our Final Year Project We are using different models like Stacked LSTM and SARIMA to predict what will be the load and how much solar energy generated . If solar energy generated is greater than load or demand then we will send that energy to commercial grid and if solar energy generated is less than the demand then we will request energy from commercial grid.
